ABOUT US!

West Texas flavored BBQ that is like no other BBQ in the Universe. Operated by Chris Beserra and employees only hand picked by him to operate a safe and healthy/clean family restaurant. Located in the hills near Mount Olympus, only one way to get to the great tasting BBQ is to take the portal, just click location on the menu for directions.


With over twenty years of BBQ experience, Chris, ensures that he continues the BBQ tradition from his father, who learned from his father. The secret and tradition of this best tasting BBQ has surpassed over five generations of BBQ techniques and good eating. There is nothing chemically added and from food to the grain of salt comes from the Earth and they will keep it that way!


The BBQ place give honor to Prometheus, not only because he was a Greek God, he was the one that gave us fire. Without fire, we could not have what we have today...BBQ! No food we cook is Greek, it is all Texas flavored.
