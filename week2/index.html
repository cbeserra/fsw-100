One of my biggest hobbies.

I am a big collector of Alien and Predator action figures. I am two figures short in having every single action figure made still boxed and never opened. I also like collecting Masters of the Universe, G.I. Joe, Thundercats, Voltron and other 80s and 70s vintage action figures. I attend comic cons all over Texas and the United States to look for rare or needed action figures to complete collections. I started collecting action figures and different toys for the past five years and have over 10,000 different sealed action figures and toys.
